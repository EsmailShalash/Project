Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Homemade Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Flatbreads for serving
  • Chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, and tahini sauce for toppings

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
2. Add chicken thighs to the marinade, making sure each piece is well-coated. Marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
3. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken thighs until browned on both sides.
5. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until fully cooked.
6. Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips.
7. Serve the chicken shawarma on flatbreads, topped with chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, and drizzled with tahini sauce.
