Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water and add yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, olive oil, and salt. Add the yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms.
3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, then place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1-2 hours.
4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a pizza pan.
6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and add desired toppings.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
